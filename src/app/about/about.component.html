Hi, I’m Vanesa – also known as Madame Pearls

Every bracelet and necklace in my shop is crafted by me with great attention to detail – from the very first idea to the final touch.

The inspiration for my jewelry came two years ago during a trip to Santorini with my husband, when he bought himself a pearl Bracelet.

Still on vacation, I started searching for lava bead suppliers and began sketching my first designs. What started as a spontaneous idea has since become my true passion: handmade jewelry that reflects character and personality – for people who love something special.
